This Privacy Policy explains how Atossa ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Atossa mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you register.
  • Health Data: Menstrual cycle dates, symptoms, weight, blood pressure, hormone levels, and other health metrics you choose to log.
  • Sensitive Information: Reproductive health information including cycle and fertility-related data.
  • Usage Data: App crash logs and performance data to improve app stability.
  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the app's core features (cycle tracking, predictions, symptom logging)
  • To personalize your experience based on your health data
  • To authenticate your account and maintain security
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (a third-party cloud database provider) with row-level security. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Atossa uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – Database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo / EAS – App infrastructure and crash reporting (Privacy Policy)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at zamaniziba18@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Atossa is not directed at children under the age of 4. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
